Cleo - Sheltie of the Month

Meet Cleo, the Sheltie sweetheart from San Francisco who embodies everything we love about our furry friends! With her dazzling smile, boundless energy, and a heart as big as the Golden Gate, Cleo brings joy wherever she goes. From frisbee at the park to cozy naps at home, she reminds us to savor life's simple pleasures. Once a hard-working office pup and now a loyal remote-work partner, Cleo is proof that with love and charm, you can thrive anywhere.
